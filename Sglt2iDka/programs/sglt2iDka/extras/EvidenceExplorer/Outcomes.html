Two outcomes cohorts will be constructed to allow for population-level effect estimation.

1. Diabetic ketoacidosis, inpatient diagnosis [DKA (IP)]

Non-technical summary: All diabetic ketoacidosis diagnoses made during an inpatient visit that are not recorded within 30 days of a previous diagnosis.
Complete specification
Initial Event Cohort
People having any of the following:
  • a condition occurrence of diabetic ketoacidosis
  • visit occurrence is any of: Inpatient Visit

with continuous observation of at least 0 days prior and 0 days after event index date, and limit initial events to: all events per person.

Limit qualifying cohort to: all events per person.

Date Offset Exit Criteria
This cohort definition end date will be the index event's end date plus 1 days
Cohort Collapse Strategy
Collapse cohort by era with a gap size of 30 days.

2. Diabetic ketoacidosis, inpatient or emergency room diagnosis [DKA (IP or ER)]

Non-technical summary: All diabetic ketoacidosis diagnoses made during an inpatient or emergency room visit that are not recorded within 30 days of a previous diagnosis.
Complete specification
Initial Event Cohort
People having any of the following:
  • a condition occurrence of diabetic ketoacidosis
  • visit occurrence is any of: Inpatient Visit, Emergency Room Visit, Emergency Room and Inpatient Visit

with continuous observation of at least 0 days prior and 0 days after event index date, and limit initial events to: all events per person.

Limit qualifying cohort to: all events per person.

Date Offset Exit Criteria
This cohort definition end date will be the index event's end date plus 1 days
Cohort Collapse Strategy
Collapse cohort by era with a gap size of 30 days.
