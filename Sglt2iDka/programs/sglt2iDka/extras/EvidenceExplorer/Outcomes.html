The outcome of this study will be the first diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA) event that occurred after the index date of an AHA therapy.

  • [DKA (IP or ER)]: The primary DKA definition will identify cases from a diagnosis code recorded in inpatient or emergency room claims.
  • [DKA (IP)]: A modified DKA definition will identify cases from a diagnosis code recorded in inpatient claims only.
