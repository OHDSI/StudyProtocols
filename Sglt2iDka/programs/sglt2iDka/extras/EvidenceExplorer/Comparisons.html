Type 2 diabetes mellitus definitions:
  • Broad definition: Diagnosis of type 2 diabetes mellitus with no diagnosis of type 1 diabetes mellitus or secondary diabetes mellitus on or before index drug exposure
  • Narrow defintion: Diagnosis of type 2 diabetes mellitus with no diagnosis of type 1 diabetes mellitus or secondary diabetes mellitus on or before or after index drug exposure, and excludes patients taking insulin monotherapy before index drug exposure and excludes patients under 40 years of age at index drug exposure
